OpenStack Cloud by RackSpace and NASA

An opensource cloud project regulated by Open Infrastructure Foundation

In July 2010, Rackspace Hosting and NASA announced an open-source cloud software initiative known as OpenStack. As of 2012 the project was managed by OpenStack Foundation. By 2018 more than 500 companies had joined the OpenStack community in 2021 it was renamed to Open Infrastructure Foundation.

https://openinfra.org

If you want to impress your clients with your cost-efficient, powerful, scalable and secured infrastructure, then hosting your own cloud using OpenStack is the best way to achieve it. When you have control of your long lasting sustainable infrastructure, production cost reduces to a minimum.

https://openstack.org
